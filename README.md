# Cat Gatekeeper 🐱

👉 https://www.catgatekeeper.org/

---

## What Is Cat Gatekeeper?

Cat Gatekeeper is a unique productivity tool designed to reduce procrastination and control screen time in a completely different way.

Created by an independent Japanese developer, the project gained attention online for its simple but powerful concept:

> If you spend too much time on apps or websites, a cat appears and blocks your screen.

---

## How Does Cat Gatekeeper Work?

Unlike traditional productivity tools that rely on reminders, notifications, or habit tracking, Cat Gatekeeper introduces a direct interruption.

When your usage exceeds a set limit, a large animated cat appears on your screen and temporarily makes it unusable.

This forces you to pause, step away, and take a real break — something most users tend to ignore when using standard focus tools.

---

## System-Wide Control

Cat Gatekeeper works across your entire system, not just your browser.

It can monitor time spent on:
- Development tools
- Design software
- Social media apps
- Messaging apps

Once limits are reached, the cat appears and stays on screen for a configurable duration.

---

## Is Cat Gatekeeper Safe?

Yes. The app is context-aware and avoids interrupting critical moments such as:
- Fullscreen videos
- Presentations
- Other immersive workflows

This ensures the experience is effective without being overly disruptive.

---

## Why People Like It

With flexible time limits, customizable break durations, and a distinctive animated design, Cat Gatekeeper stands out as a minimal but highly effective anti-procrastination tool.

Instead of trying to persuade you to focus, it enforces a simple rule:

> When the cat appears, it’s time to stop.
